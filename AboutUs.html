About Us

This NGO is a registered Section 8 Organization focusing on creating a sustainable planet for human and environment. Our mission is to establish a harmonious co-existence that sustains every life form on the planet. Through Vibhinna we would like to focus on four thematic areas: Ecology & Environment, Education, Rural Livelihood, and Health. These thematic areas help bridge the gap between humans, biodiversity, and the environment, contributing to a sustainable planet. We believe in the conservation of the basic elements of life: soil, seed, water, and air, which are essential for the existence of every life form on earth.
