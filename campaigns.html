Our Campaign

Beach Cleanup Drive

Environmental Conservation: Restoring and maintaining the natural beauty of Beaches by removing litter and debris.

Community Engagement: Bringing together volunteers and community members in a collective effort for environmental stewardship.

Objectives:

  • Cleaning up Beaches by properly disposing of waste.
  • Raising awareness about pollution's impact on marine life.
  • Encouraging future environmental initiatives.

Tree Plantation Drive

Reforestation Effort: Increasing green cover by planting native tree species.

Climate Action: Reducing carbon footprint and promoting biodiversity.

Objectives:

  • Planting 500+ trees in urban and rural areas.
  • Educating children about environmental sustainability.
  • Encouraging individuals to adopt a tree and care for it.

Food Distribution for the Underprivileged

Hunger Relief: Providing nutritious meals to homeless and low-income families.

Volunteer Participation: Engaging the community in acts of kindness and service.

Objectives:

  • Distributing meals to 300+ people weekly.
  • Partnering with local businesses to reduce food waste.
  • Encouraging long-term support and donation drives.

Education Support for Underprivileged Children

Providing Learning Resources: Supplying books, stationery, and digital tools for children.

Equal Opportunities: Supporting education for children in economically weaker sections.

Objectives:

  • Setting up free weekend tuition classes.
  • Donating books and school supplies to rural schools.
  • Encouraging mentorship and career guidance programs.
