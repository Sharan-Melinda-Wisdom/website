Tree Plantation Drive

Reforestation Effort: Increasing green cover by planting native tree species.

Climate Action: Reducing carbon footprint and promoting biodiversity.

Objectives:

  • Planting 500+ trees in urban and rural areas.

Volunteer Participation: Engaging the community in acts of kindness and service.

  • Encouraging long-term support and donation drives.

Equal Opportunities: Supporting education for children in economically weaker sections.

Objectives:

  • Setting up free weekend tuition classes.
  • Donating books and school supplies to rural schools.
  • Encouraging mentorship and career guidance programs.
